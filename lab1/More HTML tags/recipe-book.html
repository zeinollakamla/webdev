Taco Lasagna

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 45 mins
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
lean ground beef 2 pounds
taco seasoning mix 2 (1.25 ounce) packages
garlic, minced 4 cloves
chili powder 1 tablespoon
cayenne pepper ½ teaspoon
water ½ cup
corn tortillas 18 (6 inch)
jar salsa 1 (24 ounce)
sliced green onion 1 cup
sour creamn 1 (16 ounce) containe

Step 1: Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, then season with taco seasoning, garlic, cayenne pepper, chili powder and water. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

Step 3: Place 6 tortillas into the prepared baking dish. Spread 1/3 of the salsa on top of the tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the meat mixture evenly over the salsa. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the green onions. Drop 1/2 of the sour cream randomly over the green onions. Top with 1/2 cup Cheddar and 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese. Repeat layers. Top with 6 tortillas, spread with remaining salsa, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.

Step 4: Bake in a preheated oven for 30 to 45 minutes or until cheeses are melted.
