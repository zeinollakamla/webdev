Travel to Maldives

The Maldives is the most heavenly corner of our planet. From the first minutes, tourists feel the atmosphere of relaxation and unique beauty. The visiting card of the country is the beaches, covered with fine, like powder, sand. Holidays in the Maldives will not only energize, but also give a romantic mood.

Overview

Safety

  • Malé has frequent protests and demonstrations. Avoid affected areas.
  • Drug-related crime and gang-related violence occurs in urban areas, including Malé. Petty theft happens on beaches and in hotel rooms. Leave your valuables in a secure location. Monitor local media to avoid trouble spots.
  • The sea around the Maldives can have strong tidal currents. Take appropriate precautions.

Health

  • COVID-19 remains a risk in Maldives. Follow the Maldives Ministry of Health for local COVID-19 updates.
  • Insect-borne diseases are a risk, including dengue, Zika virus and chikungunya. Use insect repellent.
  • Hepatitis and rabies (including from dogs) are also a risk. Boil drinking water or drink bottled water.
